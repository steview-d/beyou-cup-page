      Why should I choose a Menstrual Cup instead of pads

      We say that it usually takes between 3 full cycles to get used to your BeYou cup. This includes finding the right fold for you or how you prefer to position yourself to insert your cup easily. It can feel a little uncomfortable at first, until you find the perfect fit, but you can always remove it or shift it around until you learn which angles to stick to.
